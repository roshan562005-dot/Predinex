3. The "Pure-Flow" User Workflow


From initial entry to long-term health optimization.

Phase 1: Secured Entry (Authentication)


Predinex supports three primary entry points:
1. Phone + OTP: Secure SMS-based login (via custom `sms.ts` gateway).
2. Google OAuth: One-tap synchronization with global identity.
3. Enterprise Email: Direct corporate/clinical login.

Phase 2: Identity Proofing (Registration)


Once authenticated, users undergo "Identity Proofing." This ensures clinical data is mapped to a verified biological identity before the metabolic protocols are activated.

Phase 3: The Command Center (Dashboard)


The core surface area where health data transforms into cinematic insight.
  • Metabolic Bio-Twin: A 3D model that breathes and rotates based on daily "Neural Core" scores.

  • Diagnostic Grid: Real-time cards for Blood Pressure, Glucose, Weight, and Sleep tracking.

  • Clinical Sync: Real-time status indicators (Database Integrity, Identity Verification).


    • Phase 4: Actionable Protocols (Daily Plans)


  • 30-Day Blueprint: A precision-timed nutrition and habit schedule.

  • AI Meal Analyzer: Users can enter meals via text or voice.

  • Neural Feedback: The app analyzes the "Glycemic Impact" and provides immediate post-meal actions (e.g., "Take a 10-minute walk").

    4. AI & "Neural Core" Intelligence


    Predinex utilizes a sophisticated Rule-Based Intelligence Engine designed to scale into full LLM integration.

    The Health Bot Assistant (`BotWidget`)


  • Neural Context: The bot reads the user's *actual* biomarkers (Glucose, Sleep, Steps) to provide personalized advice.

  • Pre-emptive Education: Explains the biochemistry of health (e.g., why walking blunts insulin spikes).

  • Session Intelligence: Maintains session memory to guide the user through their 30-day journey.


    • Meal Impact Prediction (`MealAnalyzer`)


  • Neural Database: Maps hundreds of foods (including specialized Indian cuisine like Dal, Chapati, Idli) to glycemic indices.

  • Voice Interface: Native support for high-fidelity speech-to-text and text-to-speech feedback.
